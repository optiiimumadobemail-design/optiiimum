A note from the founder

Built to do the opposite of what agencies do.

"Most agencies sell hours. We sell outcomes — and the people who deliver them sit in the same room, on the same brief, the entire engagement."

Optiiimum started in 2018 with a small studio in Cairo and one stubborn idea: that the gap between strategy and execution is where most brands lose their edge. So we closed it. Strategists, designers, engineers and media buyers work as one pod, one Slack channel, one P&L per client.

Seven years in, we've shipped 120+ brand systems across MENA, Europe and the Gulf — and the team has grown from three to nineteen without ever splitting into silos.

How we work

Three non-negotiables
that shape every brief.

We say no a lot. To bad-fit clients, to vanity metrics, to retainer creep. Here's what we say yes to — and why our retention rate is north of 80%.

01 / Velocity

Ship in weeks,
not quarters.

A brand sprint runs 4–6 weeks, end to end. We don't believe in the 6-month deck-driven engagement. The first version goes live, then we iterate against real signal.

02 / Pod model

One team,
one P&L.

Every client gets a fixed pod: strategist, designer, engineer, media lead. Same people from kickoff to launch. No handoffs to a "delivery team," no junior bait-and-switch.

03 / Outcome-priced

We tie fees to results.

30–50% of our fee is at risk against the metrics we agree on day one. CAC, LTV, conversion lift. If we miss, we eat it. If we beat it, we share the upside.
